Dinosaurs came in an array of sizes, shapes, skin colours and skin textures. They were carnivores, herbivores omnivores and scavengers. Their method of locomotion ranged from flying, swimming, or traversing land. They worked in packs and alone. Their hunting methods and biological capabilities could rival that of many modern-day creatures. The term dinosaurs is broad in a sense, almost any prehistoric animal can be classed as a dinosaur as the term is pretty much arbitrary.
